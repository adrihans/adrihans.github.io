Short bio

After growing up in Normandy, I started my higher education by going to preparatory class for the grandes ecoles in MPSI/MP at the Lycee Janson de Sailly in Paris. I was then able to integrate the Ecole Centrale de Lille where I graduated with an engineering degree in 2020 with a specialization in data science and artificial intelligence. I also did an exchange program at the Ecole Polytechnique of the Universite Libre de Bruxelles focused on data science too. I completed my education with a master's degree in public policy specialized in digital and new technologies that I am currently pursuing at Sciences Po.
